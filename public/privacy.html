Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 23, 2025

Introduction

EventSnap ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our web application (the "Service"). By using EventSnap, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Images You Upload

When you upload an event poster or flyer image to EventSnap:

  • Images are processed in real-time using Google Gemini AI to extract event information
  • Images are transmitted securely to our Firebase Cloud Functions
  • Images are NOT permanently stored on our servers
  • Images are processed in memory and discarded immediately after analysis
  • We do not create backups or archives of uploaded images

2. Extracted Event Data

The AI extracts structured information from your images, including:

  • Event title, date, time, and location
  • Event description and additional details
  • Recurring event patterns (if applicable)
  • Registration information and pricing
  • Organizer contact information

This extracted data is NOT stored by EventSnap. It is only used during your active session to populate the event form for your review.

3. Google Account Information

When you connect EventSnap to Google Calendar:

  • We receive your basic Google profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • We receive an OAuth access token to create calendar events on your behalf
  • We DO NOT store your Google password or credentials
  • We DO NOT access, read, modify, or delete existing calendar events

4. Technical & Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Time and date of visits
  • Pages viewed and features used
  • Error logs and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To analyze images and extract event information
  • Calendar Integration: To create calendar events with your explicit permission
  • Service Improvement: To improve AI accuracy and user experience
  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or technical issues
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with EventSnap
  • Communication: To respond to your support requests

Google Calendar Integration

EventSnap uses the Google Calendar API to create events. Here's how it works:

Authentication

  • We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication system
  • You authenticate directly with Google (not with EventSnap)
  • We never see or have access to your Google password
  • You can revoke access at any time via Google Account permissions

Permissions & Scopes

EventSnap requests the minimum necessary permissions:

https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events

This scope allows EventSnap to:

  • ✅ Create new events on your calendar

This scope does NOT allow EventSnap to:

  • ❌ Read your existing calendar events
  • ❌ Modify or update existing events
  • ❌ Delete any events
  • ❌ Access calendars other than your primary calendar
  • ❌ Share your calendar with others

Limited Use Disclosure

EventSnap's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage & Retention

✅ EventSnap is designed with privacy-first principles

  • Uploaded Images: Processed in memory only, NOT stored permanently
  • Event Data: Shown to you for review, NOT saved to our servers
  • Calendar Events: Created directly in YOUR Google Calendar, NOT stored by us
  • OAuth Tokens: Managed by your browser, NOT stored on our servers
  • Session Data: Cleared when you close the browser or after inactivity

We may retain anonymized analytics data for service improvement purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to you individually.

Third-Party Services

EventSnap integrates with the following third-party services:

Google Gemini AI

  • Used for AI-powered image analysis and text extraction
  • Images are sent to Google's servers for processing
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Hosts our backend functions and website
  • Provides analytics and error reporting
  • Subject to Firebase's Privacy Policy

Google Calendar API

  • Allows creation of calendar events
  • Subject to Google's Terms of Service

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connections)
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • API keys stored securely in Firebase Secrets Manager
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • No permanent storage of sensitive user data

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

Access & Control

  • Review: You review all extracted event data before it's used
  • Edit: You can modify any information before creating calendar events
  • Decline: You can choose not to add events to your calendar

Revoke Access

You can revoke EventSnap's access to your Google Calendar at any time:

  1. Visit Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "EventSnap" in the list of connected apps
  3. Click "Remove Access"

Delete Your Data

Since we don't permanently store your data, there's nothing to delete. However, if you've created calendar events, those remain in your Google Calendar until you manually delete them.

Children's Privacy

EventSnap is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

EventSnap is hosted on Google's Firebase platform, which may process data in various locations worldwide. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws.
