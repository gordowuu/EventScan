Privacy Policy for EventSnap

Last Updated: October 9, 2025

Introduction

EventSnap ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our web application (the "Service").

Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we process the following information:

  • Images You Provide: We process the images of posters or flyers you upload to extract event text. These images are processed in memory and are not stored on our servers after the text extraction is complete.
  • Extracted Text: The text extracted from your images is sent to the Google Gemini API to be structured into event data. This text is not stored by our Service.

How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the Service, which is to parse event details from an image and help you create a calendar event.

Google Calendar Integration

To provide the "Add to Google Calendar" feature, our application uses the Google Calendar API. This requires you to authenticate with your Google account.

  • Authentication: We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 system. We never see or store your Google password.
  • Permissions (Scopes): We request the minimum necessary permission to function: `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events`. This scope allows our application ONLY to create new events on your primary calendar. It does not grant us permission to read, update, delete, or see any of your existing calendar events.
  • Data Usage: The event data (title, time, location, description) you verify in the app is used to create the calendar event and for no other purpose. This information is not stored by our Service.

EventSnap's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
